# Ethics & Responsible Use

This project is intended for research, education, and legitimate image enhancement tasks only. It is not intended to be used to reverse intentional redactions, unmask sensitive data, or invade privacy.

Guidelines:
- Do not process images containing third-party private data without explicit consent.
- Log and audit processing activities in production to ensure accountability.
- Consider legal and compliance implications (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.) before deploying in production.

If in doubt, consult a legal or compliance expert before processing sensitive or redacted content.
